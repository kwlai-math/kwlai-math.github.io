I am a postdoc at Academia Sinica. You may consider me as an algebraic geometer. I am currently working on problems related to twisted K3 surfaces, K3 categories of cubic fourfolds, and Gushel–Mukai fourfolds. More about me can be found in my CV. I am currently on the job market.

Papers

In the list below, my papers on finite (algebraic) geometry are grouped together, while my remaining papers are grouped based on the examples they deal with. This isn't really a good way to organize them, as you may find, e.g., that my papers concerning Cremona transformations are spread throughout those groups.

My papers listed in a reverse chronological order can be found in my CV as well as on the arXiv. Before being published on journals, papers on this website will be updated more frequently than those on the arXiv.
