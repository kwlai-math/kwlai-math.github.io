Research

My research area is Algebraic Geometry, with a focus on birational transformations, Fourier–Mukai transforms, and algebraic varieties over finite fields. You can find my articles below and on arXiv. Preprints on this webpage are updated more frequently than their arXiv versions prior to journal publication.
