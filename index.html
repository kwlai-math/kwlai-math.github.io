Research

My research field is Algebraic Geometry. Recently, I am focusing on problems related to the arithmetic and topology of Shimura varieties arising from K3 categories. These categories include the derived category of a K3 surface, the K3 category of a cubic fourfold, and more generally the Kuznetsov component of the derived category of a K3-type Fano fourfold.
